EBESS Membership

By media on Fri, 20/02/2015
Why join EBESS?

There is absolutely no reason not to join EBESS! You'll get access to all of our events, plus tutoring and electronics. Not to mention membership of EUS, and all the perks that come with that!

2017 Events, Not to be missed!

We know that study can sometimes be challenging, so EBESS is on hand to provide you with the opportunity to socialise! This year we are bringing back some of our old favourites:

  • Dodgeball Tournament
  • Staff vs Students Soccer
  • Trivia Night
As well as providing you with some exciting new events that cannot be missed:
  • Inaugural Launch Party
  • Careers & Beers Networking Evening
  • UQIC Sports
  • and much more! 
This year, EBESS will be bigger and better than ever, so join today and don't miss out on all the excitement!

Help with your Study

Struggling with your coursework? EBESS can help! We will be running tutorials all year, with help for a wide variety of courses. There will also be a SWOTVAC revision session in both semesters for all members, to help you with your last minute exam questions.

Membership of EUS

The Engineering Undergraduate Society is THE society for all engineering students at UQ. By joining EBESS, you automatically get membership to EUS. EUS Membership gets you:

  • Free ticket to Semester 1 and 2 Launch Parties
  • Free entry to Careers Fairs
  • Members card to use at our sponsors
  • Discount tickets for Engineering Ball
  • Plus much more!

Sign me up now!

Membership for EUS & EBESS is $10. Signup for EBESS and EUS is done via the EUS website. So don't wait - Sign up now!
