About

EBESS is UQ's Electrically Based Engineering Student Society. It was formed in 2011 as a representative of students in Electrical, Electrical and Biomedical, Electrical and Computer, Software, and Mechatronics engineering disciplines within the greater UQ community.

The society provides students with the networking opportunities and academic support they need to become a well rounded engineer. Such networking events range from the casual dodgeball tournaments to professional company tours and industry interactions.

The society organises and runs workshops to mentor aspiring engineers in forming basic electrical skills, such as soldering and PCB design. Small projects are available for students to solidify their soldering skills and develop a deeper understanding of electronics.
